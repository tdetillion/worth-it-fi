Why This Site Exists

I created Worth It FI because I've seen too many smart, capable people feel paralyzed by personal finance. Not because the information isn't out there—it absolutely is. But because most financial content either talks down to you or drowns you in jargon.

Personal finance doesn't have to be complicated. It doesn't require a finance degree or hours of research every week. The fundamentals are straightforward, and once you understand them, you can make confident decisions about your money.
