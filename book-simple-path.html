The Simple Path to Wealth

by JL Collins

Best For: Beginners to intermediate investors seeking straightforward wealth-building advice

One-Sentence Summary

A no-nonsense guide to achieving financial independence through low-cost index fund investing, written as a series of letters from a father to his daughter.

Key Takeaways

1. The Simple Path

Buy and hold VTSAX (Vanguard Total Stock Market Index Fund) or equivalent total market index funds. That's it. No market timing, no stock picking, no complexity.

2. F-You Money

Build enough wealth that you can walk away from jobs, situations, or people that don't serve you. Financial independence gives you freedom and options.

3. The 4% Rule

Once your investments equal 25x your annual spending, you can safely withdraw 4% per year and likely never run out of money. This is your financial independence number.
