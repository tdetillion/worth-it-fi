When Can You Be Financially Independent?

Financial independence means having enough saved that your investments generate your living expenses. The standard rule: save 25x your annual expenses (the "4% safe withdrawal rate"). If you need $4,000/month, that's $48,000/year × 25 = $1.2M saved.
